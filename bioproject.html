ABOUT SSB

Phytochemicals of medicinal plants encompass a diverse chemical space for drug discovery. India is rich with a flora of indigenous medicinal plants that have been used for centuries in traditional Indian medicine to treat human maladies.The nondigital nature of this information limited their effective use towards new drug discovery. Hence, digitization of this knowledge into a comprehensive database on Indian medicinal plants, phytochemistry and ethnopharmacology will enable researchers to apply computational approaches towards drug discovery. In this direction, we present, SSB, a manually curated database of 322 Indian medicinal plants with their botanical name, common name, family, habitat, part used in medicines, form in which they are used, location, and therapeutic uses spanning 100’s of plant-phytochemical associations. For this we referred several databases like TCM-MeSH which is an online database for traditional Chinese medicine which captures phytochemical compositions and therapeutic uses for more than 6000 Chinese medicinal plants and Phytochemica, a database that gathered information on 5 Indian medicinal plants and their 963 phytochemicals.
